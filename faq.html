Frequently Asked Questions

What is the price?

Each lesson is €25 per hour. You can pay by Paypal or bank transfer. If you would like to book a block of lessons the per-hour price will be reduced. (see question below)

How long is each lesson?

Each lesson is 1 hour of teaching time. Any writing, reading or listening tests need to be done outside of the lesson to give you the most teacher student interaction time.

How many lessons can I book?

You can book as many lessons as you need , whether it's 1 or 20. If you do need to improve fast you can book an intensive group of lessons over a 1-2 week period subject to availabilty.

Can I get a discount if I book more lessons?

Yes, if you book a block of 5 lessons the price will be €100. A block of 10 lessons is €200.A block of 20 lessons is €400. If you need more let me know and we can arrange a similar discount.

How will the lessons be structured?

I will email you the materials and give you time to do reading wirting and listening tasks which we ill then go through during the lesson. I will give you feedback, tips and help you improve. We will also do spekaing tasks during the lesson. I will give you wiritng models to follow in order to achieve your desired band scores.

How long will it take to reach my goal?

It depends on two things. How hard you are willing to work and how much time you have to reach your goal. I believe any student can go from a band 5 to a band 7.5 with time and hard work. It will not be possible to increase your band score overnight, but in the past my students have increased their band score through dedication and a structured plan.

What materials do I need?

I will provide you with all the materials you don't need anything apart from a good internet connection and Zoom or Skype.

Can you just mark my writing and speaking?

if you just need marking services I can provide them. I still recommmend booking one lessons to go through the detailed feedback I will give you. If you would prefer written feedback I charge €10 per 4 essays.
